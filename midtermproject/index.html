Visiting Naples

Naples is a community in the Southwest of Florida known for its beautiful downtown area, white sand beaches and blue seas. For activities there are golfing, fishing, boating, water sports, arts, culture, and outstanding sunsets. Restaurants

There are many delicious restaurants in Naples. The main downtown of the city, 5th Ave, has the most popular restaurants. The most recommended restaurants there are Tulia, Aqua, Sails, Metzaluna, and Bice Ristorante.

Another great area of restaurants in Naples are in the Pavillion Shopping Center. The most recommended restaurants there Brooks Gourmet, Trattoria Abruzzo, Tacos & Tequila, and American Bistro.
